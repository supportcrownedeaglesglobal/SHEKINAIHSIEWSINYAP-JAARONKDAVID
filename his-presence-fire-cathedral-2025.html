01 The Invitation

Welcomed to the Pulpit, Not Merely the Pews

On 24 November 2025, during their Los Angeles visit for the Hollywood Walk of Fame Week, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID were received not as ordinary visitors but as invited voices at the pulpit of His Presence Fire Cathedral in Glendale, California — the international sanctuary of Dr. Gershom Sikaala.

The setting itself communicates the calibre of the invitation. Behind the pulpit rise the flags of more than one hundred nations; in front of it, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID share the microphone alongside Dr. Sikaala himself — a placement reserved, in any sanctuary of this stature, for those whose ministry the resident pastor explicitly trusts to stand at his platform.

"To be handed the microphone at a cathedral of the nations is not attendance — it is endorsement. SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID stood at the pulpit of a pastor whose covering reaches from Hollywood to the heart of Africa."

02 The Calibre of the Pulpit

Received by Dr. Gershom Sikaala

The pastor who opened his pulpit to SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID is one of the most quietly influential pastoral voices currently moving between Hollywood, Africa, and the international peace circuit.

The Pastor · The Mentor · The Author

Dr. Gershom Sikaala

Senior Pastor & Founder, His Presence Fire Cathedral  ·  Pastor & Spiritual Mentor to the late Chadwick Boseman  ·  Author · Television Host · Global Goodwill Peace Ambassador

The pastor who handed the microphone to SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID was born in Zambia, Southern Africa. Dr. Gershom Sikaala has been preaching since the age of fourteen. He is the founder of His Presence Fire Ministries, whose Glendale cathedral hosts Sunday gatherings at 333 E Colorado Street and Thursday night fire services drawing worshippers from across the Los Angeles region.

He is best known publicly as the pastor and spiritual mentor of the late Chadwick Boseman, whom he counselled through the actor's most defining years. Dr. Sikaala has also mentored other figures in Hollywood including Michael B. Jordan, and is the author of multiple titles — including Breakthrough Thinking and the Amazon #1 bestseller Look at God. It is into that same pastoral lineage that SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID were welcomed at his pulpit.

To be received by Dr. Gershom Sikaala — and entrusted with his pulpit — places SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID within a register of ministry that is neither commercial nor regional. It is the same pastoral covering under which a generation of Hollywood's most respected figures sought counsel.

03 Spiritual Continuity

A Single Week, Two Doors of the Same House

The Hollywood Walk of Fame Week of November 2025 placed SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID at two ends of the same spiritual line. They were received personally by Pastor Derrick L. Boseman, the elder brother of the late Chadwick Boseman — and only days later were welcomed to the pulpit of Dr. Gershom Sikaala, the very pastor who served as Chadwick's spiritual mentor.

In a single week, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID were received by both the family and the spiritual covering of one of Hollywood's most significant contemporary legacies. That convergence is not casual proximity. It is the kind of dual recognition that takes years of credibility to be granted, and only days to confirm.
