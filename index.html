Shekinaih Siew Sin Yap and J Aaron K David are Singapore-based faith leaders, humanitarian ambassadors, and co-founders of Crowned Eagles Global — a movement anchored in the prophetic vision of Isaiah 61, with a mandate to bring healing, liberty, and lasting transformation to communities across the world.

Over six years of dedicated service, they have built an international platform of spiritual leadership and cross-cultural diplomacy — earning recognition from the World Book of Records London, the United Nations-affiliated UNPKFC, and the Global Leadership Summit 2025 in Bangkok, Thailand.

Their story — featured as a cover story in Exquisite International Magazine — stands as a testament to what principled, courageous leadership looks like when anchored in an unshakeable calling.
