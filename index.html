At the heart of her work are cross-border business collaborations — multi-international partnerships she has forged across Singapore, the United States, India, Thailand, and the Middle East, connecting enterprises, markets, and leaders nation to nation. Her mission is to build sustainable, scalable enterprises, open new markets through international alliances, uplift communities, inspire transformation, and advance peace across borders.
