Pastor & Family · Faith Behind the Legacy

Pastor Derrick L. Boseman

Senior Pastor, New Covenant Outreach Ministries  ·  Elder Brother of Chadwick Boseman  ·  Present at the Hollywood Walk of Fame Posthumous Star Ceremony · 20 November 2025

On Thursday, 20 November 2025, Hollywood honoured the late Chadwick Boseman with a posthumous star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame at 6904 Hollywood Boulevard — in the category of Motion Pictures. The day was declared Chadwick Boseman Day in Hollywood. Tributes were delivered by director Ryan Coogler, Academy Award winner Viola Davis, and Chadwick's widow Simone Ledward-Boseman, who accepted the star on his behalf. The ceremony was reported by CNN, ABC News, NBC Los Angeles, and Yahoo Entertainment.

Beyond the spotlight of cinema, Pastor Derrick L. Boseman serves as senior pastor of New Covenant Outreach Ministries in Murfreesboro, Tennessee. Ten years older than Chadwick, he was deeply present in his younger brother's final days — and has since spoken publicly of Chadwick as a quietly spiritual man whose faith anchored his life and work.

Around the Walk of Fame week, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID attended a church service in Los Angeles where they were personally received by Pastor Derrick L. Boseman and by the resident pastor of the host church — a quiet, dignified encounter that placed the meeting not on a red carpet, but inside a sanctuary. Chadwick Boseman — best known for his iconic portrayal of T'Challa in Marvel's Black Panther — passed away in August 2020, his legacy continuing to be honoured both within global cinema and through the ministry that his family carries on.
