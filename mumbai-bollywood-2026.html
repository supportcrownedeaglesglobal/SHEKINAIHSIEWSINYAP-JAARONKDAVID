01 The Story

A Republic Week of Recognition

In the days leading up to India's Republic Day, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID arrived in Mumbai for one of the most distinguished private diplomatic gatherings in the Indian capital of cinema — the DMGP Global Diplomacy Awards, held at the Lakeside Marriott Hotel, Powai. Convened by the Diplomatic Mission Global Diplomacy (DMGP) under the leadership of its Honorary Chairman Dr. Anil Nair Thampi, the evening brought together global peace ambassadors, senior advocates of the Indian Supreme Court, Bollywood personalities, and members of the international diplomatic corps.

At the close of the ceremony, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP was honoured with the Certificate of Excellency · Global Divinity Ambassador, and J AARON K DAVID was honoured with the Certificate of Excellency · Global Peace Ambassador — both bearing the seal of DMGP, the UN Peace Keepers Federal Council (UNPKFC), and the Sustainable Development Goals, valid from 24 January 2026 to 23 January 2027.

"Ms. SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and Mr. Aaron J. David from Singapore were honored with Global Diplomacy Ambassador Certificates and medals. Both shared their vision for peace, spirituality, and global humanitarian harmony."

Published Magazine Coverage · Naim Singh, Editor in Chief

03 The Calibre of the Invitation

Conferred by the Hosts of the Evening

The certificates conferred on SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID were presented under the joint patronage of two of the evening's most distinguished hosts — each connecting the recognition to a wider international peace and diplomacy network.

The Honorary Chairman · DMGP

Dr. Anil Nair Thampi

Honorary Chairman, Diplomatic Mission Global Diplomacy  ·  Founder & Director, AN India Group  ·  Peace Educator · Global Mission for Peace and Humanity

The host who conferred the certificates upon SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID has been profiled by The Statesman India for his work in global peace, women empowerment, and cultural exchange across India. Dr. Anil Nair Thampi convenes diplomatic and cultural ceremonies in Mumbai under the DMGP banner; his developmental programmes reach across multiple Indian states; and his international network has facilitated honorary recognitions for figures across diplomacy, the arts, and humanitarian service.

To be conferred a Global Diplomacy Ambassador certificate by Dr. Thampi places SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID directly inside the inner circle of his Mumbai diplomatic register — a register that on the same evening included a Senior Supreme Court Advocate and a noted Bollywood actor.

The Returning Patron · UNPKFC

Dr. Aphinita Chaichana

Global President, UN Peace Keepers Federal Council (UNPKFC)  ·  Recipient · Mahatma Gandhi Diplomatic Award · Mumbai 2026

Already a familiar patron to SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID from their earlier reception at the Global Leadership Summit 2025 in Bangkok, Dr. Aphinita Chaichana travelled from Thailand to be present at the Mumbai ceremony — where she herself was honoured with the Mahatma Gandhi Diplomatic Award for her contribution to honouring the diplomatic missions of India. Her presence bridges the Mumbai honour to the wider international UNPKFC network that already counts SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID among its trusted ambassadors.

Republic Day · A Mission for the Poor
