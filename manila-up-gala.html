Where the World Honours Excellence

For a decade, Manila Up International Magazine has been the defining voice of Filipino and Filipino-American excellence in Los Angeles — championing leaders, artists, diplomats, and visionaries across the global stage. Its 10th Anniversary Gala at Hotel Indigo Los Angeles Downtown was a landmark sold-out evening combining a Grand Awards Ceremony, the launch of the 10th Anniversary coffee table book, and "Woven Legacies" — a haute couture showcase by master designer Renee Salud, supported by Philippine Airlines, San Miguel Corporation, PAGCOR, and the Philippine Department of Tourism.

Formally recognised by the Los Angeles Board of Public Works, the Manila Up Gala draws U.S. senators, assemblymembers, Philippine government officials, Emmy Award-winning Hollywood producers, Inside Edition anchors, and the most distinguished names in Filipino-American public life. To receive an award at this gala is to be recognised among the highest tier of Filipino excellence — on a stage watched by leaders on two continents.

SHEKINAIH
SIEW SIN YAP

The Visionary Voice Bridging Heaven and Humanity

A radiant force of intellect, grace, and conviction, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP has spent over three decades redefining how leadership, faith, and destiny converge. Appointed as "The Living Biblical Ark of the Covenant" and Founder of Crowned Eagles Global LLP and CE Global Network LLP, she has transformed divine insight into actionable strategies that empower leaders, reshape industries, and ignite transformation across continents. Her acclaimed "Behold My Messenger"™ five-volume series stands as a timeless compass for those seeking to align purpose with higher vision and to awaken the boundless potential within.

Her journey has been one of revelation, resilience, and reinvention. Through trials and triumphs, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP embodies the principle that true power is born from alignment: when conviction meets divine vision, the impossible becomes inevitable. She teaches that impact requires surrender — releasing control to allow innovation, grace, and higher wisdom to flow freely. And above all, she champions love as the ultimate currency, the invisible force that builds empires, restores harmony, and unites humanity under one purpose.

Standing at the forefront of a new era, SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP represents the rare fusion of divine inspiration and modern leadership. Her voice transcends religion and ideology; it calls every generation to awaken potential, to lead with integrity, and to design destinies that outlast time. As Manila Up International Magazine marks its 10th Anniversary, it honours a woman whose presence lights paths, whose wisdom transforms lives, and whose legacy reminds the world that destiny is not found by chance — it is designed.

J AARON
K DAVID

The Quiet Force Behind a Global Awakening

J AARON K DAVID is a man whose strength speaks through stillness. As Co-Founder of Crowned Eagles Global LLP, CE Global Network LLP and co-author of the internationally acclaimed Behold My Messenger series, he has spent decades translating divine insight into practical transformation. His leadership bridges strategy and spirit, intellect and intuition, awakening individuals and institutions alike to their highest potential. Calm, composed, and unwavering in conviction, he embodies the truth that power need not shout to be felt — it simply needs to stand firm. His partnership with SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP has ignited a worldwide movement that restores hope, integrity, and destiny across generations.

From the crucible of adversity, J AARON K DAVID rose with unyielding grace — a rebirth forged in resilience. He lives by three enduring lessons that have inspired countless leaders: strength lies in stillness; vision must be lived, not declared; and true leadership serves beyond self. For him, success is measured not by titles gained, but by lives elevated through one's guidance. These principles have become the blueprint of his life's work and the foundation of his influence across nations.

Today, through Crowned Eagles Global, J AARON K DAVID continues to mentor changemakers to transform setbacks into strategies and purpose into progress. His presence — serene yet commanding — has become a beacon of balance, integrity, and foresight in an age of noise. As Manila Up International Magazine marks its 10th Anniversary, it honours this quiet architect of transformation whose legacy proves that leadership is not defined by visibility, but by vision fulfilled. Rising from the ashes with resolute faith and refined mastery, he stands as a living emblem of rebirth, a force of resilience reborn to steady a world in motion.
