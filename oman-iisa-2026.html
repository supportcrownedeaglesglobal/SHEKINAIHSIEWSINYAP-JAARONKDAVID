The Keynote

A Voice on the Gulf Stage

SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP — keynote address at the IISA 2026 5th Edition, Sheraton Oman Hotel, Muscat.

Watch SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP · IISA 2026 Keynote · Muscat

The 5th Edition of IISA 2026 was held at the Sheraton Oman Hotel in Muscat on 12 February 2026 — organised by the I.I.I Group, Gulf Skill Pioneers, and Business Collaborations International (BCI) under the theme “Oman Vision 2040 — Global Partnerships Beyond Borders, Trade Talent & Technology.”

SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID joined an international cohort of delegates from the Philippines, India, Thailand, Canada, Egypt, South Africa, Madagascar, Poland, and the broader Gulf region — recognised on stage by an Omani host delegate alongside an Indian official, a European business leader, and a representative of the Sultanate.

A Moment Beyond the Stage

When the Spotlight Steps Aside for a Prayer

A quiet moment of prayer · Sheraton Oman Hotel ballroom

Between sessions of the formal programme, a young Omani guest in a wheelchair — Sultanate flag in hand — became the centre of an unscripted ministry moment. J AARON K DAVID leaned in to pray, while SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP sat beside in quiet presence. No cameras prompted, no introductions made.

It is a posture the founders carry into every city — recognition on the stage, service in the room.

Beyond the Awards

ILSOMAN — Welcomed into a Working Partnership

Welcome sign at ILSOMAN · Integrated Logistic Services Oman

Alongside the IISA programme, the delegation was hosted by ILSOMAN — Integrated Logistic Services Oman, an authorised economic operator and Vision 2040 partner. The visit included sessions on cross-border trade, regional logistics, and partnership pathways for Crowned Eagles Global LLP and CE Global Network LLP into the Sultanate of Oman and the wider GCC.

Hosted by Dr Shama Hussain (Founder & CEO, I.I.I Group of Companies) and Dr Aphinita Chaichana (Global President, UNPKFC) — with SHEKINAIH SIEW SIN YAP and J AARON K DAVID welcomed as honoured delegates.
